What is the course?

The course covers AI and ML concepts that are becoming indispensable in solving software problems.

Who is it for?

For people who work in software development but are not familiar with AI, ML, and have not built software solutions based on artificial intelligence.

What the course is not?

This is not a course on using AI tools for faster development. The focus is on understanding and implementing AI solutions.

Who it is not for?

For those who already know ML algorithms and use ML models, RAG, agents, and LLMs in their solutions.
